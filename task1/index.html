Our Story

Brew & Bites began as a tiny dream in a small apartment. Our founder, Tushar, was a barista who always believed that coffee tastes better when shared with love.

In 2020, during the lockdown, He started delivering homemade cookies and freshly brewed coffee to frontline workers. The smiles He received in return inspired her to open this café.

Today, Brew & Bites is more than just a café—it’s a place where strangers become friends, where students find inspiration, and where tired souls find comfort in a warm cup of kadak chai or a smooth cold brew.

Every ingredient we use is sourced from local farmers because we believe in supporting our community. From the coffee beans of Coorg to the spices of Kerala, every sip tells an Indian story.
